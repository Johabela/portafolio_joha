about

For 8 years I worked as a media planner at a well-known agency in both Colombia and Mexico. Following that, I felt I needed to explore more of the world and learn English to progress in my career, so I gave up the role in advertising and moved to Auckland to study English in an intensive full-time program. After one year, and feeling more comfortable with my English language skills, I wanted another challenge, so I moved to Sydney, changing career paths and studying childcare - I worked as a educator for two years before realising I like animals way more than children, so I quit my job and got a dog. For the past four years I have been working as a Brokerage and Rostering officer for a non-profit organisation. Now I am here, looking for another challenge - super excited about this new journey on my way to become a Web Developer.
